About Me

I am Han Liu, a final-year Ph.D student at Institute of System and Software Engineering, Tsinghua University, under the supervision of Professor Ming Gu. I received my bachelor degree in Software Engineering from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications in 2012. During my academic visit at UC Davis (03/2015-09/2015), I worked with Professor Zhendong Su. Now, I am working on cutting-edge techniques to make real-world software safer and securer. My research interests include:

  • Concurrency testing and analysis
  • Verification of interrupt-driven programs
  • Program obfuscation
