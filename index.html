About Me

I am a postdoctoral researcher at Tsinghua University, working with Professor Jiaguang Sun. Before that, I obtained my Ph.D. at Tsinghua University in 2017 under the supervision of Professor Ming Gu. I received my bachelor degree in Software Engineering from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications in 2012. During my academic visit at UC Davis (03/2015-09/2015), I worked with Professor Zhendong Su. Now, I am working on cutting-edge techniques to make real-world software safer and securer. My research interests include:

  • Program analysis/Testing (with a focus on bug/vulnerability detection)
  • Verification of interrupt-driven embedded software
  • Program obfuscation and its optimization
  • Blockchain Security
