About Me

I am a senior researcher at Oxford-Hainan Blockchain Research Institute, leading the Security and Regulation Group. Before that, I was a postdoctoral researcher at Tsinghua University, working with Professor Jiaguang Sun. I obtained my Ph.D. at Tsinghua University in 2017 under the supervision of Professor Ming Gu. I received my bachelor degree in Software Engineering from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications in 2012. During my academic visit at UC Davis (03/2015-09/2015), I worked with Professor Zhendong Su. My research interests include:

  • Secure and regulated blockchain, smart contracts
  • Formal methods for blockchain design and validation
  • Program analysis/Formal verification on software security
